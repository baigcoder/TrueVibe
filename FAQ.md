### What is TrueVibe?
TrueVibe is a next-generation social media platform that combats misinformation using AI-powered trust scores. It helps users identify credible posts and trustworthy creators in real-time.

### Is TrueVibe free?
Yes! TrueVibe is open-source and free to use. You can self-host it or use our hosted version.

### What platforms are supported?
TrueVibe is a web application that works on:
- Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Android Chrome)
- Progressive Web App (PWA) support coming soon

## ğŸ¤– Trust Scores

### What is a Trust Score?
Trust Score is our AI-powered system that analyzes content authenticity. It helps identify:
- Deepfakes and manipulated media
- Misleading information
- Authentic, credible content

### How is the Trust Score calculated?
Our AI considers multiple factors:
- Content analysis
- User history and reputation
- Community feedback
- Source verification

### Can I improve my Trust Score?
Yes! Build trust by:
- Sharing authentic, original content
- Engaging positively with the community
- Being consistent and transparent

## ğŸ’¬ Features

### How do I create a post?
1. Click the "+" button in the navigation
2. Add your text, images, or videos
3. Choose visibility settings
4. Click "Post"

### How do I send a direct message?
1. Go to the Chat section
2. Click "New Message"
3. Search for a user
4. Start chatting!

### What are Shorts?
Shorts are TikTok-style vertical videos. You can:
- Record or upload videos
- Add music and effects
- Get engagement analytics

### How do voice rooms work?
Voice rooms are live audio spaces where:
- Hosts can manage the stage
- Listeners can request to speak
- Everyone can react and chat
